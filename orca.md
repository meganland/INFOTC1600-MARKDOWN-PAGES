# Orca
Orcas are the **biggest** in the dolphin family. It can be found _anywhere_ in the world. Their activities range playing in the surf or swimming through the deep blue in every ocean across the Earth. Some common nicknames include:
-	killer whale
-	wolf of the sea
-	blackfish
